About Us

Welcome to Bubbles and Bark Dog Grooming Boutique! We are excited to provide dog washing and grooming services and believe that every pet deserves pampering in a safe and low-stress environment. We specialize in one-on-one dog washing and grooming sessions with unique handling techniques for puppies, adult, senior, and special needs dogs in stand-alone and full-services. At Bubbles and Bark Dog Grooming Boutique we don't just provide washing and grooming services, we build trust and it is our mission to build lasting relationships with both the owners and their beloved pet companions. Debora Diane Wicker-Wright is the owner of Bubbles and Bark Dog Grooming Boutique. In 2010 Bubbles and Bark opened its unique dog grooming boutique. Our professional groomers have 15 years of experience in providing dog washing and grooming services. Call us today to schedule an appointment or use our online booking system. We look forward to serving you and your beloved pet!
